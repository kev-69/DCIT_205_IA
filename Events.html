Events

Events organization are done solely based on curriculums according to the university. The department heads can also organize programs and would be considered in the year's calender.

Guest Lectures.

Occasionally, guest lectures and talks by industry professionals that students and faculty can attend are held. This broadens students minds on real life applications on the things learnt in school. For instance this semester there have been talks on the application of e-business architecture in real life industries. A guest lecturer from the Nestle Company was around to enlighten students on how courses like e-business architecture is applied in everyday industry projects. He talks on how business systems use firewalls and other technologies to protect their companies from cyber attacks. How the MS Office tools is frequently used for documentation in workplace and all that.

Such talks enables the students to know that what they are learning in school are not invain or just mere theories but are applied in real-world businesses.
